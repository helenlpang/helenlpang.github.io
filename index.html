About Me

I’ve been fascinated by computer science and mathematics from a young age, as a math and coding Olympiad competitor. I’m a USA Computing Olympiad Silver Qualifier, an AIME Qualifier, and a MIT Math Prize for Girls Invitee. I’m also Oracle-certified in Java.

However, I find the most passion in applying my technical knowledge to more tangible fields, especially software, data, finance, and media. In the past, I’ve interned at tech and fintech startups, incubators, and banks. I also have work experience in consulting, marketing, operations, and data analytics.

I'm passionate about Asian and female representation across all sectors, especially tech and entertainment. I'm currently breaking into media and entertainment management. And I’m always looking for new opportunities.
